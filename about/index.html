IDENTITY

Kalimark, a beacon since 1916, embarked on a luminous journey from Virudhupatti, now Virudhunagar. The brand’s roots deepened, and its popularity soared. The emblem transcended borders, casting a vibrant glow across the globe.
The torch was passed to the younger generation, propelling the business to new heights. In 1959, cities like Chennai, Tirunelveli, and Madurai savored the renowned Bovonto, a soft beverage that laid the foundation for Kalimark’s soft drink supremacy. Since then, Kalimark has stood firm, with Bovonto marking a pivotal moment in its history.
The journey persists, with grandsons and great-grandsons steering Kalimark to new frontiers across Tamil Nadu. Over the past decade, Kalimark has amassed numerous achievements, securing a robust market presence. With a state-of-the-art manufacturing unit, Kalimark consistently delivers remarkable output. The PET bottles emanating from the plant not only quench thirst but also foster trust among customers. The saga continues, with Kalimark leaving an indelible mark on the beverage landscape.
